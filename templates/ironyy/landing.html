Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our laundry services

Our laundry service is simple and convenient. Just schedule a pickup through our website or app, and we'll come to your location to collect your laundry. We'll clean, fold, and return your clothes within 24-48 hours, depending on the service you choose.

We currently serve all major neighborhoods in the city. You can check if we deliver to your area by entering your zip code during the checkout process. We're constantly expanding our service areas, so check back if we're not in your neighborhood yet!

We take special care with all your garments. Our team is trained to handle delicate fabrics, dry cleaning, and special care items. You can add specific instructions for individual items when you place your order, and we'll follow them carefully.

Our customer service team is available Monday through Saturday from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM. You can schedule pickups and deliveries seven days a week, with time slots available from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets including Apple Pay and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.
